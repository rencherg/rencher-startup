Inspiration

PubForum was inspired by Publius, the pen name used by Alexander Hamilton, James Madison, and John Jay to publish the Federalist Papers. Just as these founding fathers wrote anonymously to promote use of the Constitution of the United States, PubForum seeks to provide a platform where users can freely communicate with each other.
